Health Care Support

Get help when you need it most with the Health Care Support Program*, a benefit of your State Health Plan. This program complements the care you receive from your primary care provider, specialists, and other medical providers.

Get the support of a dedicated team of medical professionals — nurses, social workers, nutritionists, pharmacists, and others — can help take the stress out of dealing with:

  • Complex health conditions or Newly diagnosed illnesses
  • Lifestyle changes that lead to better health
  • Multiple providers and/or medications
  • Understanding your provider's instructions
  • Understanding your benefits
  • Short-term disability
  • Injuries from an accident
  • Making connections to helpful community resources

If you need support,we're here for you
Call 1-833-298-1069
Monday – Friday 8 am – 7:30 pm EST
Saturday 9 am – 1 pm EST

*The Health Care Support Program does not replace your current care and does not cost extra. You will continue to work with your provider(s). This program is included in your State Health Plan benefits.
