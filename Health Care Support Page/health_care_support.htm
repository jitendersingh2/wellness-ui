Health Care Support

Get help when you need it most with the Health Care Support Program, a benefit of your State Health Plan. This program complements the care you receive from your primary care provider, specialists, and other medical providers.

With the Health Care Support Program, a team of dedicated medical professionals — nurses, social workers, nutritionists, pharmacists, and others — can help take the stress out of dealing with:

  • Complex health conditions
  • Newly diagnosed illnesses
  • Lifestyle changes that lead to better health
  • Multiple providers and/or medications
  • Understanding your provider's instructions
  • Short term disability
  • Injuries from an accident

We Help You:

  • Understand your condition, your treatment options and your provider's treatment plan
  • Understand your benefits
  • Make informed decisions about your care based on what's important to you
  • Work with your providers and others on your medical team to coordinate your needs
  • Listen to your needs to help support you and the people who care about you
  • Connect to community resources

Participation in this program does NOT

  • Replace your current care. You will continue to work with your provider(s)
  • Interfere with your schedule. We work with your schedule — how often you talk to us is up to you
  • Cost anything extra. This program is included in your State Health Plan benefits

If you are dealing with a complex health condition or disease,
or one of the other items from the list above
and you feel you could benefit from speaking with a nurse, please call us at 1-833-298-1069
